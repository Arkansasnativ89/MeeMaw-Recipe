üìñ Memorandum from Wilfred Carle

April 8, 1995

These recipes are those that are in use by Alma Finken Carle. Some of them were handed down from her Mother, Clara Keller Finken, some from her Grandmother Keller; others were handed down from Wilfred's grandmothers, Caroline Nagel, and Lizzie Walters. Other recipes were from friends, recipe books and magazines.

Most of them were modified by substituting egg substitutes for eggs, vegetable oils for lard, shortening, or animal fats. Changes were also made to accommodate taste preferences.
